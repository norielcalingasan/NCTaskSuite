Plan Summary
- Free: solo core task management, reminders, basic save/load, and basic import/export with fixed task/subtask/backup limits
- Pro Monthly: everything in Free plus profiles, themes, backups, Markdown/JSON import/export, encrypted task files, premium productivity features, and Local Bridge Sharing for private local collaboration
- Business Monthly: everything in Pro plus Cloud Workspace setup, bring-your-own Firebase support, cloud member/category management, report export, online task management, and business cloud data controls

Account and Licensing Direction
- Google sign-in can load licenses already linked to an account and show them in a dropdown
- Signed-in users can save and update their account profile from `Account -> Account Profile...`, and that profile is written back to the app Firestore `users/{uid}` document
- Free-trial claims are written to the app Firestore `trialClaims/{uid}` record before the local encrypted trial license is kept on the PC
- App update configuration is stored in app Firestore `appConfig/updater` (no local `update.json` fallback)
- Signed-in updater admins can edit `appConfig/updater` from `Admin -> Updater Config...` after they are granted an editor document at `appConfig/updater/editors/{uid}`
- The bundled updater supports `manifest.json` files and per-file SHA-256 checks so unchanged files can be skipped during updates
- Signed-in shared-task members can open shared-task areas based on their assigned workspace role even if this PC has no attached paid license
- Activation uses license key + email
- Reset is request-only for now and is manually reviewed by the developer
- The app can be unlinked from the current license and returned to Free mode on the current PC
- Paid plans are monthly only for now
- Business customers can keep licensing on the developer-managed Firebase while hosting their own company task data in their own Firebase project
- Shared-task setup is split into `Cloud Workspace` and `Local Bridge Sharing`, each with separate member/category lists and plan caps
- `Cloud Workspace Tasks...` signs into the configured customer Firebase project and syncs the task board to `workspaces/{workspaceId}/tasks`
- Business setup kit includes a Firebase guide, sample client config, and Firestore rules template
- Cloud Workspace setup in the app is gated behind the Business plan

Registration Fields
- Required: email, first name, last name, mobile number, birthdate, password/sign-in method, accepted terms/privacy
- Optional: middle name, suffix, company name
- Not recommended by default: address and separate billing-only identity fields until needed

Core Task Management

- Support for subtasks, priority, categories, owners, notes, tags, and timestamps
- Multi-selection across synced task panels
- Drag-and-drop task ordering with safeguards for priority tasks

- Density and responsive layout preferences
